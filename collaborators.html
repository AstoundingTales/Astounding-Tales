Information for Collaborators

Astounding Tales is seeking disciplined visionaries, chroniclers of the unseen, and those capable of documenting the fringes of reality. We are looking for contributions that bridge the gap between speculative history and future-fact. If your work aligns with the rigors of our series—unflinching, precise, and inherently astounding—we invite you to reach out.

All correspondence by post or electronic mail to:

submissions@astoundingtales.ca

Astounding Tales does not accept unsolicited manuscripts delivered by temporal displacement, astral projection, or autonomous courier drone. We have had problems with all three.
